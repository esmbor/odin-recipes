Pesto Tortellini

Ingredients

  • Tri-color cheese tortellini
  • Pre-prepared Pesto
  • Prosciutto
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C).
  2. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil; stir in tortellini and return to a boil.
    Cook uncovered, stirring occasionally, until tortellini float to the top and the filling is hot, about 3 minutes.
    Drain.
  3. Stir tortellini, prosciutto, pesto, and 1/2 cup mozzarella together in a large bowl.
  4. Pour into a baking dish. Sprinkle remaining mozzarella over the top.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted and golden, about 10 minutes. Serve warm.
